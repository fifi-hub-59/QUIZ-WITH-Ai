**Last Updated: July 22, 2026**

We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All game progress, including XP, coins, and unlocked items, is stored locally on your device.

If enabled in the future, this app may use third-party services such as Google Play Games Services, Firebase Analytics, and Google AdMob. These services may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

### 3. Permissions
The app requires network access for potential backend integrations (e.g., fetching leaderboards or AI data).

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
